Personal ID format
ddmmyy[c][number][control_number]
  • ddmmyy : date of birt
  • [c] : century of birth: 1800 (+) 1900 (-) 2000 (A)
  • [number] : individual number if same date of birth, men have an odd, women have an even
  • [control_number] : 0123456789ABCDEFHJKLMNPRSTUVWX
